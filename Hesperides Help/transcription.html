How do I use the transcription feature ?

Sindarin is usually written using Tengwar - the letters devised by Rúmil and improved by Fëanor. Hesperides automatically displays the Sindarin words using Tengwar thanks to the Narmacil engine, by BLiZZARD. Hesperides also offers a system service to transcribe words to Tengwar.

To transcribe words using Tengwar, Hesperides needs a mode. If you don't know what that is, or if you want to transcribe non-Sindarin words, you may want to get more information about modes.
